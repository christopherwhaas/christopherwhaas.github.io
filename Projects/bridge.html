Civil and Environmental Engineering Capstone Project: Steel Bridge

This project was intended to simulate all aspects of construction, which include design, fabrication, buidling, and testing. The bridge was required to hold 2,500lb and withstand 50lb of lateral loading. The bridge was also evaluated on construction time, sustainability (i.e. resources used, total weight, associated carbon emissions, etc.), and total cost.

Our team designed a bridge that recieved the highest rating in all criteria. Our bridge spanned nearly 21 feet and stood 2 feet and 7 inches tall. All steel pieces were welded, drilled and cut by members of the team. Photos of the project are shown below.
