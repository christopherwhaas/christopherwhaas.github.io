CADcraft iOS: beta

CADcraft is a 3D iOS game that lets you walk around a 250 x 250 block grid and place/delete 1x1 cubes as you please. In this first-person, 3D builder game, you can make just about anything. After you finish creating your design, simply save and export your creation as an STL file (a 3D printable file) and share it through iOS's built-in sharing (iMessage, email, Google Drive, etc.).

Made with Swift and Apple's built in 3D API, Scenekit.
